MODDES™ Core Engine

A modular engine that turns fragmented operations into a unified, auditable flow.

▶ 102 — Schema-Discovery Engine ingest
Automatically inspects source systems to detect tables, fields, and constraints; emits structured “source maps” for downstream translation.
▶ 104 — Semantic-Mapping Engine (AI Core) align
Uses embeddings and ontologies to align semantically equivalent fields across vendors and versions; builds canonical references that survive system changes.
▶ 106 — Dynamic Translation Layer transform
Produces standardized transaction objects decoupled from vendor syntax, enabling plug-and-play across integrations.
▶ 108 — Routing & Optimization Module route
Computes target systems using fulfillment scores and business rules; supports retries, backoff, and circuit breaking.
▶ 110 — Feedback Learning Module learn
Captures performance and reconciliation feedback to fine-tune mappings, routing thresholds, and validation rules over time.
▶ 112 — Finance & Audit Layer audit
Ensures double-entry integrity across updates; emits immutable audit trails and variance alerts to a parent ledger.
▶ 114 — ERP Systems (Input) sources
Inbound events from ERPs, MRPs, CRMs, and operational apps; adapters provide normalization, health checks, and retry policies.
▶ 116 — Parent Ledger (Output) destinations
Central financial record updated after reconciliation; exports evidence to BI and compliance systems.
