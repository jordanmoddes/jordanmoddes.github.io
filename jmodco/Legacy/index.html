Who we are

A small project for a big problem: aging, distance, and confusing tech.

Jmod Legacy is a focused piece of Jmod Co. — built around one question: how do we make it easier for families to support aging loved ones without turning them into unpaid IT support?

1
Life has spread us out.
Families are often states or countries apart. “Checking in” now means:
  • Calls, texts, emails, portals, apps, and passwords.
  • Older adults juggling devices and accounts they never asked for.
  • Caregivers trying to manage everything remotely.
2
Caregivers become the help desk.
The adult child or grandchild often ends up:
  • Explaining the same steps again and again.
  • Sorting out “is this safe to click?” every week.
  • Doing all of this on top of work, kids, and their own life.
Services

A calm layer between your family and the chaos of technology.

Legacy is being designed as a simple, guided “companion” that can explain, remind, and organize — without asking your loved ones to learn one more complicated system.

A
Plain-language explanations when things look scary.
When a parent gets a letter, email, or pop-up they don’t understand, Legacy is meant to:
  • Break it down in clear, direct language.
  • Flag what’s urgent vs. what can wait.
  • Suggest practical next steps for them or for you.
B
Gentle structure for the “important details.”
Over time, Legacy aims to help your family keep track of:
  • Key contacts and recurring appointments.
  • Household “how to” information only they know.
  • Simple check-ins that show someone is watching out for them.
Companion

Early-stage concept — built around clarity, not features.

The Legacy Companion is being shaped as a calm, always-available guide you can ask questions like:

  • “How do I explain this letter to my dad without scaring him?”
  • “My mom got this email — is it real or a scam?”
  • “What’s a simple way to check in without making them feel watched?”
α
Where it is today
Behind the scenes, Legacy uses the same engine as other Jmod Co. tools. Here we’re tuning everything toward:
  • Slower pace and simpler wording.
  • High-contrast visuals for easier reading.
  • Controls that feel safe to both seniors and families.
FAQ

Questions you might already be thinking about.

Is this a medical, legal, or emergency service?

No. Jmod Legacy is intended as a supportive, informational tool — not a replacement for doctors, attorneys, or 911. It’s meant to help you understand and organize, not to make critical decisions for your family.

Will our family’s information be sold or shared?

The long-term vision for Legacy is privacy-first: minimal data, clear controls, and no hidden reselling. As features are defined, this page will include a plain-language description of how data is handled — not just fine print.

Do my parents or grandparents need to be “good with technology”?

No. Legacy is being built specifically for families where the older generation is not comfortable with technology. Much of the interaction may come through you, the family member — at whatever pace feels right.
