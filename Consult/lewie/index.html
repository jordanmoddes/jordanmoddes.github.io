Our Story

Lewis Holmes III, known affectionately as “Lewie” by friends and fellow outdoorsmen, has spent a lifetime honing his skills and expertise in various outdoor pursuits. From archery competitions that have taken him to national and international stages to mastering the art of fishing and hunting in the serene wilderness, Lewie’s love for outdoor sports runs deep. 2

With a dream of sharing his passion and knowledge with others, Lewie and his family established Lewie’s Unlimited Outdoors. The store is not just a business. It is a reflection of their values, their experiences, and their dedication to building a strong community of outdoor enthusiasts.

When customers step into Lewie’s Unlimited Outdoors, the goal is simple: give them exceptional service, real expertise, and a shopping experience that leaves them confident and ready for the next trip, hunt, or competition. 4
