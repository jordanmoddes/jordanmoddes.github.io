Our Story

Built on Hard Work
and Honest Prices.

Lopez Tire Shop has been serving North Austin drivers from our location right on I-35 for years. We're not a chain. We're your neighbors — real people who know what it means to need a tire fixed fast without getting taken for a ride on price.

We carry a wide selection of new and used tires for cars, trucks, and SUVs. Whether you blew one on the highway or you're setting up all four, we'll get you rolling the same day — usually the same hour.

What We Do

Full-Service Tire Shop

New Tires

We carry a solid selection of new tires across major brands and sizes for cars, trucks, vans, and SUVs. Ask us what's in stock — we keep inventory moving so you always get a fair deal.

Quality Used Tires

Need to keep it budget-friendly? Our used tires are hand-inspected for safety and tread. A great option when you need reliable rubber without the new-tire price tag. Venta de llantas usadas.

→ Starting at great prices

Tire Balancing

Vibration at speed, uneven wear, or that shimmy you've been ignoring — our balancing service gets your wheels running smooth and extends the life of every tire. Balanceo disponible.

→ Same-day service
🔧

Flat Tire Repair

Nail in the tread? Slow leak you can't find? We patch and plug flats fast. Pull in and we'll have you assessed within minutes. No appointment, no hassle.

→ Walk-ins welcome
🔄

Tire Rotation

Rotating your tires regularly is the single best way to extend their life. We'll move them in the correct pattern for your vehicle and flag anything that looks off while we're at it.

→ Quick turnaround
🚘

All Makes & Models

Sedans, pickups, work vans, full-size SUVs — we've seen and serviced them all. Bring whatever you drive. If we have your size, we mount and balance it on the spot. Cash only, thanks.
